Why we built Maruflow

We couldn't find software that kept up with us. So we built it.

Maruflow was created in conjunction with Circles of Hair, one of Australia's leading salon brands. After years of running a genuinely busy, high-volume salon, we kept hitting the same wall: every booking system on the market did the basics, but none of them connected the dots between the chair, the client, and real business growth.

So we built the platform we always wished existed — one that handles everything a busy salon needs day-to-day, then goes further by integrating deeply with the tools that actually grow a business. Maruflow is technology that feels like a joyful partner in craft, not a cold, intimidating system. It's the modern artist's accomplice.

This is salon software designed by salon people, for salon people who refuse to settle.

Frequently asked

What is Maruflow? +

Maruflow is an all-in-one salon booking and growth platform: bookings, online booking, automated reminders, two-way messaging, advanced booking rules, an automated reviews & reputation engine, and advanced reports with revenue forecasting — plus deep HubSpot and Zapier integration for true end-to-end client journey tracking and marketing.

Who is behind Maruflow? +

Maruflow was created in conjunction with Circles of Hair, one of Australia's leading salon brands. It was built to solve real problems we faced running a busy, high-volume salon — when no existing system could meet our needs.

Can I buy Maruflow now? +

Not yet — Maruflow is currently in private early access and isn't open for public purchase. Join the early-access list and we'll reach out as soon as a spot opens for your salon.

How deep is the HubSpot integration? +

Very. Maruflow connects bookings and client activity directly to HubSpot so you get accurate end-to-end journey tracking, and can market at key moments with both automations and manual interventions. Zapier support extends this to 6,000+ other apps.
